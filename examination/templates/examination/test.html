Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • The test consists of multiple-choice questions.
  • You have a total of 60 minutes to complete the test.
  • Each question carries equal marks.
  • Make sure to submit your answers before the time runs out.

Test Structure

The test is divided into three sections:

  1. General Knowledge
  2. Technical Skills
  3. Problem Solving

Each section has a different number of questions, so please manage your time accordingly.

FAQs

1. Can I pause the test?

No, the test cannot be paused once started. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection before beginning.

2. What happens if I lose my internet connection?

If you lose your connection, try to reconnect. However, any unanswered questions will not be saved.

3. Will I receive my results immediately?

Results will be available within 24 hours after the test completion.
